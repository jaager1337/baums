What data we collect

We only collect personal data that you choose to give us. When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name

We use the information you send only to respond to your enquiry, prepare a quote, and discuss and deliver a possible commission. The legal basis is your consent and the steps taken at your request before entering into a contract (GDPR Article 6(1)(a) and 6(1)(b)).

Who we share it with

We do not sell or rent your data. We share it only with the service providers that help us run the site and reply to you:

  • Vercel, our website host.

These providers may process data outside the European Union under appropriate safeguards. We do not share your data with anyone else.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access your data, correct it, delete it, restrict or object to its use, receive a copy, and withdraw your consent at any time. To exercise any of these, email info@baumstein.eu and we will respond.

If you believe we have handled your data improperly, you may also complain to the Latvian Data State Inspectorate (Datu valsts inspekcija).

Cookies

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. The only thing we store in your browser is your chosen language, which is functional and never shared. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this policy first.
