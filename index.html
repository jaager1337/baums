How It Works

From Vision
to Heirloom

Four stages. Six to eight weeks. One table made entirely for you.

I

Consultation

We discuss your space, your vision, your materials — in person or by call or chat.

II

Design

Technical drawings and visualisations prepared for your approval. You see exactly what will be built before a single cut is made.

III

Crafting

Hand-built in our Latvian workshop. The same craftsmen work the piece from first cut to final polish. We own every stage of the process.

IV

Delivery & Installation

We deliver personally and install your table ourselves.
