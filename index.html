The Craft

Every table starts with an idea.

We combine carefully selected hardwoods with natural stone to create furniture designed specifically for your space. Every piece is built to order, shaped by hand, and made only once.

We source each material for its quality, character, and longevity — so every table is as individual as the home it belongs in.

How It Works

From Concept
to Completion

Every table is made to order through a simple four-step process.

I

Consultation

We discuss your space, style, dimensions, and material preferences — in person, over the phone, or online.

II

Design

We prepare detailed drawings and realistic visualisations so you can approve every detail before production begins.

III

Craftsmanship

Your table is handcrafted in our Latvian workshop by the same team from start to finish, ensuring consistency and attention to detail throughout the process.

IV

Delivery & Installation

Once complete, we deliver and install your table, making sure everything is exactly as intended.
