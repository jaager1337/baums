Our Philosophy

No two Baumstein tables
have ever been the same.

Every piece is conceived in conversation with you — and built entirely to your specification, from first sketch to final polish.

Baumstein was founded on a simple belief: that a table is more than furniture. It is the centre of a home — where meals are shared, decisions are made, and memories are formed.

We source only the finest European hardwoods and hand-select each stone slab for its unique character. Your table will never be catalogued, numbered, or repeated.

Made entirely to order. No exceptions.
I
The Wood

European hardwoods — Oak, Walnut, Ash, Smoked Oak — each hand-selected for its grain, colour, and character. Your choice shapes the warmth of the entire piece.

II
The Stone

Black Marquina, White Carrara, Verde Guatemala, Travertine, Golden Onyx. Each slab is unique — chosen by you, set by our craftsmen, never replicated.

III
The Form

Pedestal, trestle, four-post, arch — dimensions, edge treatment, leg design. Every structural decision is yours to make. We execute it to perfection.

Tell us what you imagine.
We will build it exactly as you dreamed.

How it works

From Vision
to Heirloom

Four stages. Six to ten weeks. One table made entirely for you.

I
Week 1

Consultation

We discuss your space, vision, and materials — in person or by video call. No catalogue, no templates.

II
Weeks 2 – 3

Design

Technical drawings and material samples prepared for your approval. You see exactly what will be built.

III
Weeks 3 – 10

Crafting

Hand-built in our Latvian workshop. The same craftsmen work the piece from first cut to final polish.

IV
On completion

Delivery

White-glove delivery and placement into your home. We don't leave until every detail is right.
