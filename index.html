The Craft

Every table begins as a conversation.

The wood determines the warmth. The stone determines the character. Together they become something that could only ever belong to you — never catalogued, never repeated.

We source only European hardwoods, hand-selected for grain and colour. Every stone slab is chosen for its unique veining — the same slab will never appear in another table.

How It Works

From Vision
to Heirloom

Four stages. Six to ten weeks. One table made entirely for you.

I
Week 1

Consultation

We discuss your space, your vision, your materials — in person or by video call. No catalogue, no templates. Just a conversation.

II
Weeks 2 – 3

Design

Technical drawings and material samples prepared for your approval. You see exactly what will be built before a single cut is made.

III
Weeks 3 – 10

Crafting

Hand-built in our Latvian workshop. The same craftsmen work the piece from first cut to final polish. We own every stage of the process.

IV
On Completion

Delivery & Installation

We deliver personally and install your table ourselves. We don't leave until every detail is exactly right.
